Syncro Monsters

Syncro Monsters are placed in the Extra Deck, seperate from the Main Deck. You Special Summon a powerful Syncro Monster to the field instantly by using the Levels of your monsters.

Tuner Monsters for a Syncro Summon

In order to Syncro Summon a Syncro Monster, you need 1 Tuner(look for "Tuner" next to its type). The Tuner Monster and the other face-up monsters you use for th Syncro Summon are called Syncro Materials. The sum of their Level of Syncro Monster you can Summon.
