All Spells and Traps Weren't Created Equal

Not all Spell and Trap Cards work exactly the same. Some have Icons that give them certain powers. To become a master Duelist, you to be able to identify

Spell and Trap Card Icons
Spell Card(oo) Trap Card(oo)
Continuous-These Spells and Traps stay on the field after you play them. Their effects work as long as they are on the field.
Spell Card(+)
Equip-When you an Equip Spell, choose a face-up maonster. The Equip Spell stays on the field to power it up! If the monster goes away, so does the Equip Spell.
Spell Card(/)
Quickplay-You can play these Spell Cards from your hand during any phase of your turn. You can also Set them and use them like Trap Cards.
Spell Card(*)
Field-These Spells go in the Field Spell Zone and stay there.
Spell Card(@)
Ritual-When you have a Ritual Spell Card, along with the matching Ritual Monster Card, in your hand, and the required Tribute (as listed on the ritual spell card), you can activate the Ritual Spell Card, be placing it in the Spell & Trap Zone.
Trap Card(<-)
Counter-These Traps negate the effects of other cards when used right away.
